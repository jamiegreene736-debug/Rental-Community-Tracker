## STEP 2 — Add Guesty credentials to Replit Secrets

In the Replit Secrets panel (not .env file), add:
  Key: GUESTY_CLIENT_ID      Value: [from Guesty dashboard]
  Key: GUESTY_CLIENT_SECRET  Value: [from Guesty dashboard]

Then, in the entry point of the app (index.js, main.jsx, server.js,
or wherever environment variables are exposed to the frontend), add:

  window.__GUESTY_CLIENT_ID__ = process.env.GUESTY_CLIENT_ID;
  window.__GUESTY_CLIENT_SECRET__ = process.env.GUESTY_CLIENT_SECRET;

If this is a full-stack app with a backend, it is STRONGLY recommended
to proxy the Guesty auth call through your backend instead of exposing
credentials to the browser. In that case:
  - Create a route: POST /api/guesty-token
  - That route calls the Guesty auth endpoint server-side
  - Update the `getAccessToken()` method in guestyService.js to call
    your /api/guesty-token route instead of Guesty directly
